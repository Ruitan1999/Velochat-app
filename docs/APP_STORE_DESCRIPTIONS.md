## Full description (Google Play — up to 4000 characters)

```
VeloChat keeps your cycling club organized and connected. Plan group rides, share routes, and chat in one place—so you spend less time coordinating and more time on the bike.

PLAN RIDES TOGETHER
• Create rides with date, time, distance, and location
• Add a route: upload GPX/FIT files or import from Strava
• Invite your club or hand-pick riders
• RSVP in one tap so everyone knows who’s in

CHAT THAT KEEPS UP
• Dedicated chat for each ride—no digging through group threads
• Real-time messages with push notifications so you never miss an update
• Create extra chat rooms for your club whenever you need them

CLUBS & RIDERS
• Join or create clubs and see rides from your crew
• Connect with other riders and build your cycling circle
• Optional Strava link to pull in your saved routes

BUILT FOR RIDERS
• Clean, simple design so you can schedule and chat without the clutter
• Push alerts for new messages and ride updates
• Profile with optional photo and bio—you choose how much to share

Whether you run a weekend club or just want to organize rides with friends, VeloChat makes it easy. Download free and get your next ride on the calendar.
```
